La Sallian Values and Prayers

Spirit of Faith

Let us remember that we are in the Most Holy Presence of God.

Ating alalahanin na ang Diyos ay ating kapiling.

Zeal for Service

I will continue, O my God, to do all my actions for the love of you.

Pagpapatuloy ko, O Diyos, ang lahat ng aking gawain upang ikaw ay lalong pakaibigin.

Communion in Mission

Live Jesus in our hearts. Forever!

Mabuhay ka Hesus sa aming mga puso, magpakailanman.
