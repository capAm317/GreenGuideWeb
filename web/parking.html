On Campus Parking

For students who drive to school or those who are waited for by their cars. There are many places where you can park. Prices vary but range from Php 30 to Php 50 for fixed rate parking.

Manila Campus
Sport Complex Parking
Br. Andrew Gonzales FSC Hall Parking

Note: You may buy parking tickets from the Accounting Office. Tickets are sold by bundles of ten, each ticket costing Php 30.

STC Campus
Students' Parking
Milagros R. Del Rosario Building

Visitor's Entrance

Off-Campus DLSU Parking

There are other parking locations around Taft, Manila Campus. But before you do, make sure that you dont leave any valuables inside your car, make sure that you know the person in charge of the parking spaces, and make sure they are trustworthy.
