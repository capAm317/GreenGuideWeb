Dean's Message

Our dear Lasallian froshies,

Welcome to De La Salle University!

In our tradition of teaching minds, touching hearts, and transforming lives, allow us to accompany you in your journey as college students through the Lasallian Personal Effectiveness Program (LPEP).

LPEP will introduce you to our University, its culture, and the Lasallian core values we constantly uphold in all the things that we do here. It will give you a glimpse of what your college life will be for the next three to four years. Make sure you enjoy your LPEP!

LPEP is made more exciting this year with the launch of this Green Guide app, prepared especially for your batch by your fellow Lasallians from the College of Computer Studies. The app contains more information about the student life here in DLSU. With just one click, you will find the different offices, groups, and organizations that you may join to make your college life more productive and exciting.

Lastly, as you start your journey in DLSU, I am happy to introduce you to a community that takes care of your welfare, looks after you, and ensures the development of your whole potential. We commit ourselves to molding you to become Lasallian achievers for God and country.

I thank God for bringing you all here in DLSU.

Animo La Salle!

Amelia Galang

Dean of Student Affairs
